Open Roles

Middle/Senior Java Developer (Fintech)
Remote, Kyiv, Odesa
We’re looking for someone who has:
  • Experience in products with 30-day retention> 8%.
  • Experience in conducting in-depth interviews.
  • Experience in building push/email/in-app notification chains.
  • Understanding the principles of viral mechanics.
  • Worked in Amplitude or similar analytics systems.
  • Experience in running and validating A/B test results.
What you’re gonna do?
  • Increase the conversion of current products systematically.
  • Proactively monitor the competitive environment.
  • Generate and validate new product ideas.
  • Manage the resources required to implement the idea.
  • Assess the impact of product functionality on the overall financial performance of the company.
We offer:
  • Convenient schedule for larks, owls, and those who haven’t decided yet: from 8 (12) to 17 (21).
  • Office in the city center (Odesa, Kyiv) or the ability to work completely remotely.
  • Paid vacation and sick leave.
  • Opportunity for training and development: the experience and assistance of our employees, assistance in professional adaptation, and training by the curator throughout the trial period.
  • Career growth within the company, which you can influence yourself.
  • No micromanagement: we evaluate the result of the work.
  • Participation in conferences and seminars organized by the Netpeak Group.
  • Gamification system, netpixes, and Netpeak Store (an internal store where you can choose anything from socks to iPhones).
  • Corporate events and entertainment, both at the level of the entire Netpeak Group, and more locally, at the level of Saldo Apps.
  • Work in a local product that is known all over the world.
